Dogs, your friends forever

Hello, everyone, I am Nikku owner of Nikk kennel Faridabad, A multi-super specialty kennels and Pet store, Faridabad. I am breeding dogs for the last 22+ years and a behaviorist for dogs. I like to my experience and knowledge that I have gained in my career so that we can provide a better life to these companions who love us unconditionally and remain loyal to our families throughout their lives. I always try to share my experiences and thoughts on different breeds of dogs so that we can give them a better life, to understand their emotions and basic requirements. Hope all of you will love it and inspire me for the betterment of these best friends forever. The Dogs we Breed are very well maintained , well groomed , very friendly and Social.
